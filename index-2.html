FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

When is the launch date?
We are launching our first collection on October 13th.
How many Nyolings are there?
There are a total of 7777 Nyolings living in Nyoland.
What are the minting details?
Public mint will be able to mint their Nyolings in a fair-dutch-auction style, starting at 0.03 ETH and progressively going down to a resting price of 0.01 ETH. Nyolisted people will afterwards be able to mint their Nyolings for free.
How many Nyolings can I mint?
Nyolist: 1 free per wallet
Super Nyolist: 2 free per wallet
Public: 3 per wallet
How do I mint a Nyoling?
Through the 'Mint Now' button on our official website. To guarantee yourself a mint, you will need to be Nyolisted.
